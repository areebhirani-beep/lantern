## Voiceover, timed to the film

**[0:00, the flame]**
> "My grandmother dreams in a language I was never taught."

**[0:04, the loss]**
> "Every two weeks, somewhere in the world, a language goes quiet for the last time. Hers is on that list."

**[0:12, we write it down]**
> "So we sat with her, and wrote down everything she could still remember. Forty-one phrases. Then we gave them to a tool we built, called Lantern."

**[0:17, what it does, say this clearly]**
> "Here is what Lantern does. It takes those few words, works out the grammar hidden inside them, and builds a real course to learn the whole language from. Think of Duolingo, for a language Duolingo will never make."

**[0:31, the honest rule]**
> "And it never makes up a word. It can only teach what she actually said. Anything it cannot be sure of, it throws away."

**[0:38 to 1:13, the live demo plays on screen]**
Narrate over the recording as it happens. Rough beats:

> "Then it built a course. Flashcards, real pronunciation." *(the Learn tab, ~0:57)*

**[1:13, the scale]**
> "She is not alone. There are three thousand languages like hers, waiting. Lantern has room for every one."

**[1:20, the close]**
